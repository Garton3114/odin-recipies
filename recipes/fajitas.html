Fajitas

Description

A spectacular recipe for the most versatile food in the world

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Red onion
  • Peppers
  • Can of sliced tomatoes
  • Harissa Spices

Steps

  1. Heat a wok on medium heat with a small amount of oil
  2. Slice chicken into strips and place in the pan to cook until slightly golden
  3. Slice peppers and onions and add to the wok
  4. Once chicken cooked add tomatoes and harissa spices
  5. Mix all ingredients and turn wok to a high temperature, stir until water from tomatoes has evaporated and mix has thickened
  6. Serve and enjoy!
